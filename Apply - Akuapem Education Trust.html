2025 Scholarship Program Guidelines

Akuapem Education Trust Foundation has been established to enhance education and capacity building in Akuapem and beyond.

The Foundation’s scholarships are available for brilliant but underprivileged students within the Akuapem Ridge.

Applicants must:

* Be a native of Akuapem. 
* Have gained admission to a public university.
* Show demonstrable need for support with a 
   personal statement. 

Scholarship funds will be paid directly to the University and not to the student. 
Students will be responsible for submitting tuition and other fees to the Foundation promptly.

The form should be accompanied by:

* Copy of admission letter into public university
* Copy of National ID 
* Copy of transcripts/WASSCE results 
* Statement of purpose/Letter of motivation
* 2 Letters of recommendation
* CV (Curriculum Vitae)
