About Us

AET is a foundation whose core mandate is to sponsor higher education for bright, hardworking and underprivileged individuals on the Akuapem ridge. The Trust seeks to provide funding for the provision of human capital, educational infrastructure and facilities within the Akuapem locale.

I am not just a student of books but a student of life. There have been moments where doubt attempted to creep in, but I stand here fuelled by the unwavering belief that the seeds I plant today will bear fruit tomorrow. I have hope and faith that every effort I make, every door I knock on, and every challenge I face is a step closer to fulfilling my personal legend and achieving my dreams. I will not relent until that day comes.

Today, I don’t say “I made it” as a solitary cry of victory but as a chorus. To every teacher, mentor, family member, and friend who poured into me, I say, “We made it.” This is our story, our triumph, our celebration.

And so, with every clap, every tear of pride, and every smile of encouragement in this room, I echo a resounding truth: “Truly, I have made it.” Thank you.

FAQs

Who qualifies to apply for AET funding?

High school graduates who:

a. Are natives of Akuapem.
b. Have gained admission to a public university.
c. Show demonstrable need for support with a personal statement.

When can I apply?

As a matter of practice, applications are accepted at the beginning of each academic

year and closed just before the academic year starts.
You are encouraged to visit our website frequently and also follow us on our Social Media platforms to stay up to date on application openings and what we have been up to.

How do I apply?

Visit the application page to learn more about the application requirements and to access the application form.

How many scholarships will be awarded?
  1. AET awards a varied number of full-tuition scholarships in each application round, based primarily on availability of funding and the quality of the applicant pool.
  2. One of these scholarships is dedicated to a student who has gained admission to study Akuapem Twi at the tertiary level.
Will AET fund my tuition if I gain admission into a private school or an international school?

No. Currently, the foundation only sponsors students who have gained admission into Ghanaian public tertiary institutions.
